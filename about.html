About Custom Dining

Making Dining Safe for Nigerians with Dietary Restrictions

Our Story

Custom Dining was born from a simple yet powerful observation: millions of Nigerians living with diabetes, food allergies, and other dietary restrictions struggle to find safe dining options when eating out.

In Nigeria, where food culture is rich and diverse, dining out should be a joy, not a source of anxiety. Yet for those managing diabetes, celiac disease, or other health conditions, restaurant meals often feel like a gamble with their health.

Our Mission

We believe every Nigerian deserves to enjoy dining out safely, regardless of their dietary restrictions. Custom Dining bridges the gap between health-conscious diners and restaurants that care about their customers' wellbeing.

How We Help

  • Diabetic-Friendly Options: Find low-sugar, low-carb meals across Nigeria
  • Verified Nutrition Data: Every meal reviewed by registered dietitians
  • Local Restaurant Partners: Work with restaurants in your LGA
  • Smart Filtering: Search by calories, carbs, sugar content, and ingredients
  • Personalized Recommendations: AI-powered suggestions based on your health goals

Our Impact

Since launching, Custom Dining has helped thousands of Nigerians:

  • Discover safe dining options in their local area
  • Better manage their diabetes through informed food choices
  • Enjoy eating out without compromising their health goals
  • Connect with restaurants that prioritize customer health

Our Team

Our team includes healthcare professionals, nutritionists, software developers, and food industry experts who are passionate about improving health outcomes for Nigerians through better food choices.

The Future

We're continuously expanding our network of partner restaurants across Nigeria, adding new features to help users make informed dining decisions, and working with healthcare providers to integrate Custom Dining into diabetes management programs.
