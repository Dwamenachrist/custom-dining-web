Why We Built Custom Dining

Food is essential—but for Nigerians living with diabetes or dietary restrictions, dining out can feel unsafe or confusing.

Custom Dining exists to make eating out easier, safer, and smarter. We partner with local restaurants, dietitians, and food experts to bring personalized meal planning right to your phone.
